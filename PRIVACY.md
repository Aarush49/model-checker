# Privacy Policy for ModelCheck
Last Updated: April 13, 2026

1. **Data Collection & Personal Information**
ModelCheck does not collect, track, or store any personal information or usage telemetry. We do not have access to your files, model configurations, or API keys.

2. **Third-Party API Services**
ModelCheck allows users to send requests to third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, or local server endpoints).
    - **Transmission**: Your data is sent directly from your device to the API provider you have configured.

    - **Storage**: We do not proxy or intercept this data. Your API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to the developer.

    - **Policy**: Please refer to the privacy policies of your chosen API providers to understand how they handle your data.

3. **Model Downloads**
When downloading models, the application connects to external repositories (such as Hugging Face). These third-party platforms may log standard technical data, such as your IP address, as part of the download process.

4. **Local Processing**
Whenever possible, ModelCheck prioritizes local execution using ONNX Runtime. In these instances, no data leaves your machine.

5. **Contact**
For questions or support, contact the developer on [GitHub](https://github.com/neeleshpoli/model-checker/issues).
